About Me

My passion for nature and photography

Hello!

As a nature and photography enthusiast, I enjoy exploring Turkey’s breathtaking natural landscapes and capturing these moments through my camera. Mountain hiking and outdoor activities are among my main interests.

Uludağ holds a special place for me. With every hike I take there, I discover a different side of nature. Snowy peaks, forest paths, and stunning sunsets… Each photograph tells its own story.

This website was created to share both my photography journey and what I have learned throughout my Web Design course. I hope my photos allow you to feel the beauty of nature as well.

My Interests

  • Nature Photography
  • Mountain Hiking and Trekking
  • Landscape Photography
  • Outdoor Sports

Why Did I Create This Website?

To Share My Photos

I wanted to present my photographs in an organized way and share them with others.

To Learn Web Design

I wanted to learn how to design a modern website using HTML and CSS.

To Promote Love for Nature

I aimed to increase people’s interest in nature and raise awareness.
