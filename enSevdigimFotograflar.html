The photographs on this page consist of moments that hold a special place among all the images I have taken so far. Each one is not just a landscape, but a memory that reflects my emotions, the atmosphere of the moment, and the experience I lived through.

While capturing these favorite photos, I mostly focused on seizing the moment rather than planning the shot. A beam of light hitting the landscape at the right angle, the slow dispersal of fog, or an unexpected silence made these frames unforgettable for me.

These photographs represent a calm evening by the sea, a summit reached during a mountain hike, or the untouched beauty of nature. What makes them my favorites is not technical perfection, but the feelings they gave me at the moment they were taken.
